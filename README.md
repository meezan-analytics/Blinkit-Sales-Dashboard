Interactive Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard: A comprehensive data visualization project analyzing $997K+ in total sales. This dashboard tracks KPIs across 14+ product categories, outlet tiers, and customer ratings to identify growth opportunities and consumer preferences in the quick-commerce sector.
# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Business Overview
This dashboard provides a 360-degree view of Blinkit's sales ecosystem. By analyzing nearly $1M in sales data, the report identifies which item types, fat contents, and outlet sizes contribute most to the bottom line.

## 🚀 Key Metrics (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** $997.16K
* **Total Items Sold:** 7.06K
* **Average Sales per Transaction:** $141.24
* **Average Customer Rating:** 3.92 / 5.0

## 📈 Deep Dive Insights

### 1. Product & Fat Content
* **Top Categories:** Fruits and Vegetables, Snack Foods, and Household items lead in volume.
* **Preference:** Regular fat products dominate the market share at **64.67%** ($4.57K) compared to Low Fat products at **35.33%** ($2.49K).

### 2. Outlet Performance
* **Location Strategy:** **Tier 2** locations are the top performers with **$393.15K** in sales, followed by Tier 3 ($341.42K) and Tier 1 ($262.59K).
* **Outlet Size:** Medium-sized outlets contribute the largest share of revenue (**37.83%**).

### 3. Historical Trends
* **Establishment Growth:** The analysis shows a significant peak in outlet establishment around **2015**, maintaining a steady performance through 2022.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Visualization Tool:** Power BI / Excel
* **Features Used:** Slicers for Item Type, Donut Charts for Proportion Analysis, Funnel Charts for Location Tiers, and Trend Lines for Time-Series Analysis.

## 💡 Recommendations
* **Focus on Tier 2:** Since Tier 2 outlets are outperforming others, marketing efforts should be doubled in these zones.
* **Inventory Optimization:** Increase stock for "Fruits and Vegetables" as they are the primary traffic drivers.
